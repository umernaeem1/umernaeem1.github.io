I am a PhD student in Economics with research interests in **labor markek** and **econometrics**. My work focuses on evaluating large-scale interventions and building data pipelines to understand economic impacts.

### Research Interests
* **Labor Markets:** Analyzing game theoretical models to understand labor market outcomes.
* **Environmental Policy:** Analyzing the impact of regulations on energy consumption and sustainability.
* **Development Economics:** Experience with Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs) and large-scale evaluations.
* **Data Science:** Building automated data pipelines and predictive models using Python.

### Background
Before starting my PhD, I worked in management consulting for government agencies and served as a research assistant on several policy evaluations. I am currently working on bridging the gap between economic theory and data-driven decision-making.

Outside of research, I enjoy exploring new ways to manipulate data with Python
